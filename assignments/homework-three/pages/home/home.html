Indianapolis City Market
The Intersection of culture and culinary goodness.
The Original Gathering Place
As the gateway to Monument Circle and the crown jewel of the up-and-coming East Market cultural district, the Indianapolis City Market provides an experience like no other! Stroll through this market house that's long been a part of Indiana history. Pick up something for supper. Find that unique gift. Satisfy the inner foodie in you. Hang out with family and friends. Or simply be. Whether you're a longtime Hoosier or a first-time visitor, you're sure to find good food and good times at the Indianapolis City Market. Visit today and be a part of what's happening at Indy's Original Gathering Place!

Explore the amazing beneath the familiar. The Indianapolis City Market and Indiana Landmarks offer guided descents into the City Market catacombs, a long-hidden ruin beneath City Market's Whistler Plaza.
